Introduction

I am a 2nd year undergrad student at NTUA School of Architecture. This is the website i created for "ARCH 4342 Computational Methods in Creative Arts and Sciences", a course that belongs in the 2nd semester's program.
A little about me is that i like to sketch, meditate, go for walks and travel. Sightseeing, visiting museums, exploring new places and trying different foods is extremely exciting for me. Most of all, i love learning about the architecture and the buildings everywhere i go.
In this webpage, i will be presenting my work: all the projects i did for the course during this semester.
